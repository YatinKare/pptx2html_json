• Factors Affecting Hb-O2 Curve
  • For each factor, ask yourself whether the change will result in a greater need for O2 in the tissues. “Yes” -> right shift.
  • Factors affecting O2-Hb affinity
    • ↓ pH (acidosis) → right shift
    • ↑ PCO2 (suggests hypoxemia) → right shift (Bohr effect)
    • ↑ temperature → right shift
    • ↑ [2,3-DPG] in RBC → right shift
